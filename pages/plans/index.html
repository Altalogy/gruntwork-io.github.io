Subscription & Support

What is the Gruntwork Subscription?

Join the Gruntwork Community, a collection of companies that are all built on the same Infrastructure Packages, with support and maintenance from Gruntwork. As a subscriber, you get access to all updates, improvements, fixes, and security patches to your Infrastructure Packages via a simple version number bump. You also get access to the Gruntwork team of DevOps and infrastructure experts for support, maintenance, questions, and code and design reviews.

Features

Access to all updates, improvements, fixes, and security patches for you Infrastructure Packages.

Access to the Gruntwork team for support, maintenance, questions, and code and design reviews.

Proactive maintenance. Periodic audits and improvements of your infrastructure.

Security alerts. We monitor major security advisories and notify you of critical vulnerabilities.

Monthly Newsletter. We notify you of updates in the DevOps world and our packages.

Discounts. Subscribers get discounts on Gruntwork Infrastructure Packages and training.
