Why do we have these terms?

Gruntwork customers often find value in working with a Gruntwork partner to help them further customize or more deeply understand the Gruntwork product. These terms were designed by Gruntwork to serve as a fair contract between a Gruntwork customer and Gruntwork partner. To make these terms effective, you'll need to sign an SOW with a Gruntwork Partner that references these terms, and in that SOW you can mutually customize or override any terms you want.
