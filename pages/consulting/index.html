Consulting

We believe traditional AWS consulting is obsolete. Instead of charging hourly to build the same thing over and over again from scratch, our goal is to capture the solutions to all common infrastructure problems as code, in the form of Infrastructure Packages.

We offer consulting only for the setup & customization work it takes to get you up and running with the Infrastructure Packages. If we don't already have an Infrastructure Package that does what you need, contact us, and we'd be happy to build it—and, of course, document it, test it, and support it.
