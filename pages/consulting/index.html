Consulting & Training

Consulting

Whereas many consultants try to maximize their hours, at Gruntwork, the only way we can make our company scale is to get as much leverage out of our Infrastructure Packages as we can. Therefore, our incentive is to keep consulting hours to a bare minimum—usually, just the initial setup, customization, and training tasks necessary to get you up and running quickly.

If you would like us to develop something custom that isn’t already in our Infrastructure Packages, we’re happy to do it so long it solves a generic problem that many other companies have too, and can therefore be turned into an Infrastructure Package. For example, we won’t help you develop your proprietary app code, but if you need a new type of monitoring system, a different Docker orchestration tool, or a different way to do load balancing than what we already offer, we’d be happy to build it—and, of course, document it, test it, and support it going forward.
