What we do

At Gruntwork, we've taken the thousands of hours we spent building infrastructure on AWS for and condensed all that experience and code into pre-built packages that we offer to clients. These packages are:

  • Proven. Take a look at the list of clients using Gruntwork packages in production. Instead of wasting months reinventing the wheel and hacking things together with duct tape and glue, take advantage of infrastructure that has been assembled by experts, follows best practices, and has proven itself in the real world.
  • Tested. Each package goes through a series of automated tests before release. Each release follows SemVer principles.
  • Documented. Each package comes with thorough documentation on how to use it. We also offer training on DevOps technologies and best practices for your team.
  • Customizable. We work with your company to adapt the packages to your needs. We get most clients up and running on AWS in about 2 weeks.
  • Supported. We offer a subscription service to on-going bug fixes and security updates for your packages, as well as access to all new packages that we develop.
  • Yours. The code for all the packages lives in your codebase, so you can use and modify it however you want, forever. There is no vendor lock-in, no hidden fees, and no incomprehensible magic.
